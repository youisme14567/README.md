# UniWell- Student habit tracker App
SDG3: Good Health and Well-Being

# problem description
UniWell is a application that is designed for students to monitor their health habits of drinking water, sleeping , exercising and eating. This application will support the SDG3 goal of  Good Health and Well-Being as it helps will help the students to achieve a healthy lifestyle.

# SDLC Overveiw:
The team completed the following tasks:
- Planning & Requirements (Sprint 1):
The team worked on planning and requirements.The product vision was defined based on SDG3. SDG3 is all about health and well-being. Then we worked on getting the functional and non-functional requirments so  you to know the basics of of what the app is supposed to do and how it is supposed to perform. The trello backlog was all setup.
  
- Analysis & Design (Sprint 1):
The team designed the Use case diagram to see how the system would interact with eachother and the Relationship between eachother. Then the wireframes was sketched out and created into a full mockup using to see all the screen and buttons.
  
- Prototype Implementation (Sprint 2):
The team created an interactive prototype using Figma. The key features were implemented for the app, including authentication, health logging, dashboard summaries, and reminders, etc. The Trello was aslo updated to reflect the work completed.
  
- Testing & Evaluation (Sprint 2):
The team completed the usability test with students. The feedback was also completed to refine the prototype for the clarity and usability. The team prepared presentation slides, rehearsed delievery, and compiled the final report.
